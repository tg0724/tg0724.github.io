Recipe of
Horse's Neck

2 parts brandy
6 parts ginger ale
1 dash angostura
ice cubes

Fill up the collins glass with ice
Pour ginger ale, brandy into the glass
Stir together
Add angostura drop by drop
Garnish with lemon spiral
