Recipe of
Peppermint Kiss

1 part Midori
1 part vodka
3 parts pineapple juice
0.5 parts lemon juice
0.5 parts peppermint schnapps
ice cubes

Fill up the glass with ice
Fill up the shaker with ice cubes
Pour peppermint schnappslemon juicepineapple juicevodkaMidori into the shaker
Shake well
Strain into the collins glass
Garnish with raspberry mint leaves
