Recipe of
Manhattan

1 dash angostura
1 part red vermouth
2.5 parts canadian whisky
ice cubes

Fill up the mixer glass with ice cubes
Pour angosturared vermouthwhiskey into the mixer glass
Stir together
Strain into the cocktail glass
Garnish with cocktail cherry
