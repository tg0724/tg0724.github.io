Hey! Welcome to C.BAR. On the left side, you can find your perfect fit cocktail by simply moving your mouse to the color you like or the alcohol level you preferred. The special part of C.BAR is that the classification of cocktails is based on the characteristics. Under the Sweeties, most of the cocktails are in low alcohol level and are juice or fruit based. The Hero section is for heroes who would like to challenge high alcohol level. Also, the most classic cocktails are belong to the nostalgia section. Cocktails attracts lots of people just by the fancy color. So, if you are the one who like to try different colors of cocktails, you should definitely check the Color Lover section out. Enjoy your C.BAR tour.
