Recipe of
Rum Punch

2.5 white rum
1 part cherry liqueur
2.5 parts pineapple juice
1 part orange juice
0.75 parts lime juice
0.5 tsp cinnamon
ice

Add ice to the shaker
add white rum, pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, cinnamon,cherry liqueur to the shaker
shake well
add ice to the collins glass
strain into the glass
garnish with raspberry
