Recipe of
Strawberry Mojito

1½ parts white rum
1½ parts strawberry liqueur
1 part sugar syrup
1 part lime juice
3 parts club soda
4 pieces mint leaves
ice cubes

Add sugar syrup, mint leaves to the collins glass
Crush sugar syrup, mint leaves with muddler
Fill up the glss with ice
Pour lime juice strawberry liqueur, white rum,into the glass
Fill up the glass with club soda
Stir together
Garnish with strawberries, lime wedges
