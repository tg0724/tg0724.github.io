Recipe of
Tequila Sunset

3 parts tequila
1 part brandy
4 parts orange juice
ice cubes

Fill up the collins glass with ice
Pour tequila into the glass
Fill up the glass with orange juice
Pour brandy into the glass
Garnish with cocktail cherry
