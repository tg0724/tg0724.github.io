Recipe of
Sex on the Beach

2 parts vodka
1 part peach schnapps
1 dash grenadine
2 parts orange juice
2 parts cranberry juice
crushed ice

Fill up the collins glass with ice
Pour peach schnapps, vodka into the glass
Fill up the glass with cranberry juice, orange
Add grenadine to the glass
Garnish with orange wedge
