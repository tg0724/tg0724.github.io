Recipe of
Hurricane

1 part lemon juice
1½ parts white rum
1½ parts overproof rum
1½ parts dark rum
1 dash grenadine
1 tbsp sugar syrup
3 parts orange juice
ice cubes

Fill up the shaker with ice cubes
Add lemon juice, grenadine, sugar syrup,orange juice
Shake well
Fill up the hurricane with ice
Strain into glass
Garnish with orange wedge
