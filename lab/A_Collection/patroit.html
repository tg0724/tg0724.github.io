Recipe of
Zombie

1 part white rum
1 dash angostura
1 part gold rum
1 part lime juice
1 part pineapple juice
0.5 parts overproof rum
1 part dark rum
crushed ice

Fill up the glass with ice
Fill up the shaker with ice
Pour pineapple juice, orange juice, gold rum, angostura, white rum into a shaker
Shake Well
Strain into the hurricane glass
Garnish with pineapple, orange
