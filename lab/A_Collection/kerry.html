Recipe of
Kerry Cooler

2 parts irish whiskey
1.5 parts sherry
1 part oregat syrup
0.5 parts lemon juice
1 part club soda
ice cubes

Fill up the glass with ice
Fill up the shaker with ice
Pour lemon juiceoregat syrupsherryirish whiskey into the shaker
Shake well
Strain into the collins glass
Fill up the glass with club soda
Garnish with orange wheel
