Bantjes started working in the field of visual communication in 1983 and worked as a book typesetter from 1984-1994. She became well known as a talented graphic designer from 1994-2003, when she was a partner and senior designer at Digitopolis in Vancouver. She is famous for the Valentines' series. But the poster like this is specially for her. This work is made for AGI congress. The work is made up by several rectangles in white, yellow and gray with different shades. The overlapping effect makes the typography stands out. The typography is strong and all in capital. The typography is original work have many shades of shadows. One in white and one in black. It is hard to see what's going on with the background so it automatically cause people's curiosity.
