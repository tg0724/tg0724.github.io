Information is Important


My word for the theme is "information". The first interpretation is that design should be informational. I think that a successful design should satisfy the aesthetic and function requirements at the same time. Due to the fast grow of the world, especially the people in the cities, e fficiency is one of the significant things being cared about. In the past, people may would like to read 1000 words to know what is happening world, but now people are more like to read short news, infographics, pictures or even bullet point outlines to know what is happening because they care about time consuming. So, how to make a website(only talk about web and app for now) that can make people know the information at the first glance is also important. After some researches, I found that the clear hierarchy, appropriate fonts, structured layout and well-chosen pictures can all contribute to improve user's experience. From design perspective, as a designer, I always like to add some decoration based on my personal will. But for a website, it is more user based. Too much decorative element or animations might overwhelm the users and take the place of the real information that should e delivered to users. In this way, every element that appear on the website should has its own reason.

The second step is design "information". Not only for the elements showed on the website, the information on the webpage should also be designed. I think designer should be able to deal with huge information and convert the dry data to a vivid design. No one would like to read dry numbers, codes. A way to do it to transform the date to infographic, tables, pictures, video, i nteractive apps and so on. Then I look more to information design, which is similar to data visualization. We are live in a big data world. Big data is a new concept. What can big data do is that it concludes the history data, get a conclusion and estimate the future. There are three kinds of data for now but none of them belongs to art area. In my personal opinion, I do no think that big data will influence the art itself but will definitely useful to estimate the value of the art. What another thing it can do is that it enable designers to get a better understanding of users preference based on the data analysis, which is super helpful for interaction design--an area deign based on human.

Just for now, I think I will focus on how to design information, how to transfer the data, information to designs and how to make it interactive with users. But I will definitely dig more into the algorithms and big data in art area more.
