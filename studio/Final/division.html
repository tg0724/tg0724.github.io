Online School Structure

The ideal situation for the online school is that it functions as the regular school, which means it has the hierarchy and the basic structures. People who are going to manage the school should belong to the management department. Under the principal, they are also directors, advisors, and faculties.

Letâ€™s talk about the management department first. The first thing is to have a principal to manage the whole team. The second thing is to divide the duties. Academic Department is going to take care all things about academic. Security Department is going to caring for the whole website. For example, the environment of the chatting room, whether it is a peace environment for children to communicate or not. The last part might be service department that might focus on arrange courses based on time, manage books or other donations, etc.

The directors in online school are in charge of getting resources for classes. As said before, the courses are provided by volunteers or by donations from academic organizations. So, the duty of directors is to make sure that those courses meet the requirements. For example, whether the content of the course is appropriate? Whether the contents are taught clearly enough so the students could gain understanding? Another thing they need to do is to make sure those individual volunteers are qualified. The other standard character in school is advisors. They provide necessary information for students, answer most of the questions students have and help them plan their course. So, the advisors in the online school should be familiar with all the information about the school and have strong communication skills so they could communicate with people in different backgrounds. The most important role is the faculties. They are the people who directly to communicate with children. Even though it is an online school, their responsibilities should not be changed.

The other possibilities are to have a department that made of parents. Those are the people who care about their children most. They have rights to give suggestions. In this way, parents are going to communicate with each other. (It is impossible to make every parent do that in a poor area, but it is possible to have representation in each area.)

Ever single role that mentioned before is based on volunteers. It should not relate to money at all.
