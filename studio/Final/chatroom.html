Functionality of Chatroom

Generally speaking, when people mention the chat room, what will be in their mind is small chat. But in here, the most important and most basic function of chat room is to exchange information. This chat room can have the various countries, regions, and the children who learning through Internet platform connected together. We all know the meaning of the school is not only the acquisition of knowledge, but also the exchange and communication between peers. So for an online school, the chat room has played an important role in communication. In other words, on this platform, people who chat with you may share interesting things with you at any time. For example, children living in the desert told the children living by the sea that they have to go all day to fetch some water, or tell each other a different cultural customs and festivals. This function allows them to break the regional pattern of traditional school, so it is a good way to let them broaden their horizons.
