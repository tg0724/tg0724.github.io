Online School Structure

In the case of ensuring the level of education, my goal is to improve the varieties of the courses as much as possible. The whole website site will focus on basic education, but at the same time, I also hope to provide students with some interesting education. In my last blog, I mentioned that most of the courses are completed by volunteer teachers or donations from charities. So the courses' systematization and integration will be an important goal that need to be assessed.First there are two aspects will be explored and discussed. Basic education refers to the most basic education, such as basic mathematics, English and other subjects,which can be applied to our life. For poor countries, basic education should be limited to knowledge, also the technical training should be provided. Such as basic medical knowledge, emergency protection knowledge etc. In terms of interests, such as music, dance, painting and other interests. These courses are designed to provide children with more interesting life and bring them more hope. However, what is the most important is the basic education of necessary skills. Since the most courses are online resources and or provided by some volunteers, a course director in online school should assume the responsibility of course reviewing. So as to ensure that courses published online are qualified and fully beneficial to education. Also, how to update and continue the education is really important as well. Different countries have different situations, which means that different area might need different kinds of education. For example, the first goal for them is to recognize Chinese characters not English letters. So, how to make courses be various and fit for the different situations is difficult. In this case, the website is depends on volunteers in a world wide zone.
