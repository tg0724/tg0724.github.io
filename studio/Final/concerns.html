Concerns and Difficulties

For such an idealized online school, there is a lot of difficulties and impossibilities, such as there is no network support in a really poor place. If it is possible to save online content to local file and regularly update. Or using satellite technology with other countries' support. While doing online school, I often think which one is important between knowledge and skills. If it is more important to tell them how to build water pipes rather than tell them how to learn mathematics. Until one day, I suddenly understood it is always better to teach a person who are hungry to fish than to give him some fish.Although skills are essential, what can change their difficulties still is their own and knowledge they learned. Also if the online school can really be built up, which is not only for those children who can not go to school, but widely applied in society. If people study in online school and through some test they can get the related degree,does this will change the educational patterns and forms that continued for thousands of years? This is also a question that worth thinking.
