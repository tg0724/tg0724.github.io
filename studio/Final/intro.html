Intro to Online School

Based on the statistics, there are 30 million children are growing up in poverty, which means in low income community, there was only one book for every 300 children. In US, there are 25 states that had poverty rates for school-age children that lower than the national average. The lack of the education resources directly influenced the accessibility of basic education for the children. The worse thing is that the dropout rates of 16 to 24-years-old students who come from low income families are seven times more likely to drop out than those from families with higher incomes. All the data show the urgency of issue. The situation would not be change unless the environment of education gets better. It is hard to to get over the poverty in short time. But a child only have limited time for basic education in their life before they get shaped. So there is a way that might solve the problem. Living in a technology era, there are so many ways to get information from online. It is true that it is impossible to let 30 million attend schools physically but it is possible for them to attend the school online. The online school (different from online courses because it has a conception of community) provide all the information and the basic education course. These could be math, basic skills, literature, writing, etc. Unfortunately, for now, most online courses are in commercial side and most of them are for adults. However, the online school in its setting is only for children, who are hard to access the school resources. The online school has the conception of "online community", where students can talk about everything happened in their life, so they would not stay in their own world. Since it is a public school, no money should related to the project. Instead of trying to give every kid a book, it is more possible to donate a computer for a neighbor. All the courses are online and there also could be connections between each neighborhood, or across the states or even in world scale. It is not a evil if children do not have book. The most terrible thing is that children cannot get the resources because where they born or who they are. Education should be fair to everyone and the ultimate goal is that everyone has the same right to access the education. The online school is a good start for children to get basic education and border their views through the virtue community.
